Plan

There are 4 types of plans direct stock plan, dividend reinvestment plan, discount/full-service broker, and stock fund. The purpose of plans are to determine what you gonna do with the stocks. Note: as a stock trader, you will mainly focus on discount/full-service broker, those are people who help you buy and sell stocks.

Direct Stock Plan
- Buying & selling stocks directly through the company
- Often limited to employees, shareholders, minimum purchase (refers to ammount of purchase, account level (refers to programs or systems like the VIP system)
- Can only buy & sell stocks at certain time
- depends on what plan the company offered and what you choose
Dividend Reinvestment Plan
- Allows you to buy more shares of stock by reinvesting dividend payment to the company
- Need to sign an agreement with the company
Discount/Full-Service Broker
- People (often called brokers) buys & sells stocks/shares to customer for a fee
Stock Fund
- Mutual fund that invests primarily in stocks
- Offered by investment company
