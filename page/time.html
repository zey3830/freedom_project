Market Time

Opening & Closing
- Most market open locally around 9am to 10am and close around 4pm to 5pm
- NYSE, NASDAQ, TSX (Toronot stock exchange), the Shanghai stock exchange all open at 9:30am
- Depending on the market, they might have lunch breaks and closed on holidays
Crashes
To prevent market crash, the circuit breaker method are used (currently the following markets are using it, NYSE & The Chicago Mercantile Exchange and the Investment Industry Regulatory Organization of Canada (IIROC))
- 10% drop before 2pm, market close for 1 hour
- Between 2pm & 2:30pm, market close for 1 hour 30 minutes
- 20% drop before 1pm, market close for 2 hours
- Between 1pm & 2pm, market close for 1 hour
- After 2pm, market close for the day
- 30% drop anytime of day, market close for the day
